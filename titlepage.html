This dictionary is based on the characters definitions and references found in each of the three books of the Warlord Chronichles trilogy by Bernarn Cornwell and is intended to be a companion look-up for characters, places and trivia that the reader will find throughout.

I have enhanced the entries with citations and small historical additions when deemed appropriate, being however careful to avoid disclosing references to characters' deeds that the reader would discover later in the books.
