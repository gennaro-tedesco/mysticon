Bridgeburners
The Bridgeburners were a legendary elite division in the Malazan 2nd Army who were respected by the majority of the Malazan Empire and even their enemies. The unit symbol was a stone arched bridge, lit with ruby flames.

"First in, last out." (Motto of the Bridgeburners)

"A Bridgeburner—such a strange name for soldiers, and yet . . . so perfect there in the chasm between the living and the dead." (Kalyth)

Ben Adaephon Delat
Mage of the 9th Squad, Bridgeburners, originally from the Seven Cities continent. Black-skinned and tall with a lean face and fine ascetic features, he had a row of neat, smallish, white teeth. His arms were scrawny, and his long hands were almost feminine and also described as thin and spidery. He wore leather armour over a heavy quilted tunic, thin wool undergarments, leather leggings, and moccasins. His voice took on a strange lilt whenever he struggled against speaking his mind. He had a habit of licking his lips when nervous.

Whiskeyjack once stated that while Kruppe might have been the most intelligent man alive, Quick Ben was but a short step behind.
