Apt
Aptorii demon bound to Kalam Mekhar; rescues thirteen hundred crucified Malazan children, merges with the blind boy Panek, and shadows Kalam through the Imperial Warren while tearing apart the Semk-bound warrior that Pearl confronts.
Baudin
Massive former heavy infantryman and hidden Talon sent by Tavore to guard Felisin; loses an ear protecting her during the Cull, breaks curfew to kill guards, strangles Beneth during the Whirlwind uprising, and dies buying time from the D’ivers Gryllen after freeing Felisin and Heboric.
Beneth
Korelri slave who rises to crime lord at Skullcup on Otataral Island; plays both sides with Commandant Sawark, keeps Felisin as addict and lover, and is finally killed by Baudin during the rebellion.
Blistig
Commander of the Aren Guard left to hold the city while Coltaine marched; resentful of Pormqual’s indolence yet loyal to the Empire, he endures the siege as the Chain of Dogs collapses at the gates.
Coltaine
Wickan Fist of the Malazan 7th Army, crow-cloaked cavalry commander who forges the Chain of Dogs escorting tens of thousands of refugees from Hissar across Seven Cities to Aren with relentless tactical ingenuity and brutal discipline.
Duiker
Imperial Historian from Dal Hon assigned to Coltaine’s 7th; veteran soldier-turned-chronicler who documents the Chain of Dogs from Hissar to Aren, riding with the column and fighting when needed.
Felisin Paran
Youngest Paran sister seized in Laseen’s Cull; enslaved to Otataral mines, survives Skullcup with Heboric and Baudin, is hardened by betrayal, and later takes up the mantle of Sha'ik Reborn in Raraku.
Heboric Light Touch
Ex-High Priest of Fener with severed hands and body of tattoos; exile turned historian who escapes Skullcup with Felisin and Baudin, gains ghostly jade-veined hands and prophetic sight after touching a jade pillar in Raraku, and serves as reluctant guide to Sha'ik Reborn.
High Fist Pormqual
Indolent, jewel-draped High Fist ruling Seven Cities from Aren; delegates power to priest Mallick Rel, hoards luxuries while the rebellion rages, and ultimately betrays Coltaine by withholding relief at the Chain of Dogs’ end.
Icarium
Half-Jaghut wanderer of immense but forgotten destructive power; travels Seven Cities with Mappo seeking his lost past while drawn toward the Path of Hands convergence.

"I admit, Mappo, I feel close this time. Very close." (Icarium, seeking his past)
Iskaral Pust
Madcap High Priest of Shadow at Tremorlor; duplicitous schemer serving Shadowthrone, guides Icarium and Mappo through the Azath while muttering to his mule and spying via his wives’ coterie.
Kamist Reloe
Gaunt High Mage of the Army of the Apocalypse, thought dead in Raraku but revealed leading rebel hosts; constructs the ramp at P’atha, hurls sorcery at the Chain of Dogs, yet is repeatedly outmaneuvered by Coltaine’s Wickans.
Keneb
Pragmatic former Malazan captain demoted to supply officer; joins Coltaine’s march, keeps the refugee train moving, and later commands within the battered column.
Korbolo Dom
A High Fist of the Malazan Empire, he was a half-blood Napan with shaved, dusty blue skin covered in scars. He turned against the Malazan Empire, joining the rebellion in Seven Cities and leading the Whirlwind's warriors. His decision to become a renegade stemmed from his ambition and perceived slight when Coltaine was given command of the 7th army over him, as Dom was considered too bloodthirsty to lead.
Kulp
Squat, white-haired Meanas cadre mage of the 7th; ferries to Skullcup to extract Heboric, survives the Silanda and an undead dragon, then is consumed by the D’ivers Gryllen after safeguarding Felisin’s band in Raraku.
Leoman of the Flails
Sha'ik’s chief bodyguard and battlefield commander; famed for twin flails and desert-honed discipline, he orchestrates rebel maneuvers around Raraku and survives Coltaine’s pursuit.
List
Young Wickan-trained corporal of the 7th Army; serves under Bult and Nil, conveying orders and witnessing the attrition of the Chain of Dogs.
Lostara Yil
Pardu-born Red Blades captain with tattooed skin and wide black eyes; sent from Ehrlitan to hunt Kalam, partners uneasily with Claw agent Pearl through the Imperial Warren, and is seized in Aren amid suspicions of treason.
Mallick Rel
Jhistal priest and scheming advisor to High Fist Pormqual; manipulates Seven Cities politics, quietly supports the rebellion, and later betrays Pormqual to Korbolo Dom.
Mappo Runt
Trell guardian sworn to keep Icarium’s past suppressed; loyal, grief-haunted companion who steers Icarium away from triggers while traversing Seven Cities and the Azath at Tremorlor.
Minala
Keneb’s widowed sister-in-law travelling with her children on the Chain of Dogs; sharp-tongued and resilient, she forms a strained alliance with Duiker and the Wickans.
Nil
Wickan warlock twin, alongside Nether; channels earth spirits to shield the Chain of Dogs and coordinates with Sormo E’nath under Coltaine.
Nether
Young Wickan warlock twin who partners Nil; her healing and spirit-work sustain refugees and soldiers through the march’s attrition.
Pearl
Pearl was a Mockra mage of the Claw, tall, dark-skinned and sharply handsome with green eyes, favouring loose green silks and rings of glittering green gems; one-quarter Tiste Andii, he carried hidden throwing stars on his chest-belt and knives beneath his half-cloak, operated under the alias Salk Elan, and was once Topper's favoured lieutenant. He escorted Lostara Yil through the Imperial Warren in the hunt for Kalam.
Pella
Skullcup guard and soldier who guides Felisin’s group through the Otataral camp upheaval before meeting his end in the desert.
Ryllandaras
Soletaken and D’ivers known as the White Jackal; shifts between giant man-jackal and a pack of wolves, notorious predator along the Path of Hands and feared curse of Quon and the Seven Cities hinterlands.
Sawark
Commandant of Skullcup on Otataral Island; brutal overseer complicit in abuses, toppled during the Whirlwind revolt as the mines erupt in chaos.
Sha'ik
Sha'ik is the title for the chosen one of the Whirlwind Goddess, also known as the "Hand of Dryjhna" and "Seeress of the Apocalypse." The original Sha'ik was a prophetess and leader of the Whirlwind rebellion in the Holy Desert Raraku. She was a small, dark-haired woman whose appearance reflected her harsh desert life.
Sormo E'nath
Reborn Wickan warlock, outwardly a lanky boy in a goat-cowled cloak yet carrying the souls of elder warlocks; leads Nil and Nether in support of Coltaine, communes with land-spirits to counter Kamist Reloe, and dies under arrows at Vathar Crossing.
Tavore Paran
Newly appointed Adjunct to the Empress; orders the Cull that seizes her sister Felisin, later maneuvers Imperial forces from Malaz while remaining unaware of Felisin’s fate in Raraku.
Toblakai
Huge, taciturn warrior guarding Sha'ik’s camp; a soletaken-linked giant on the Path of Hands, later revealed as Karsa Orlong, though in this tale known only by the Toblakai name.
